meatballs

Description

This will teach you how to make swedish meatballs, but just the balls and not the gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 dl Milk
  • 5 msk bread crumbs
  • 500 g mincemeat
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsk salt
  • 1 krm pepper
  • 1/2 tsk sugar

Steps

  1. Mix milk and breadcrumbs. Let it sit 10 min.
  2. Add mincemeat, onion, egg, salt, pepper and sugar. Mix well.
  3. Moisten your hands with cold water and make the meatballs with your hands.
  4. Fry the meatballs in butter or dietary fat
