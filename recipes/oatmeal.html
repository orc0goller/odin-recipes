oatmeal

Description

In this recipe i will teach you how i make my oatmeal. It's delicious, fast and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 dl Oats
  • 2 dl Water
  • 1 dl Milk
  • 1 tsk Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 dl Berries

Steps

  1. Add oats, water and salt to your pot
  2. Heat it on stove on medium heat for 6 min
  3. Add egg and stir until mixed
  4. Add Banana and berries
  5. Let it sit for 3 min
  6. Add milk
